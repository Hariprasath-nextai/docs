**SearchGPT.dev** is an advanced platform that leverages AI to transform search experiences across different sectors. Our suite includes three specialized services designed to cater to unique user needs:

## AskGPT
This LLM-based chatbot is ideal for anyone seeking quick, accurate answers about your product. Whether you're looking up information or need assistance, AskGPT provides reliable, AI-powered answers.

## AgentGPT (Beta)
AgentsGPT is an agent-driven assistant that can connect with various sources and perform autonomous actions using real-time data, providing quick LLM-driven insights to end-users. It's ideal for businesses seeking autonomous tasks with dynamic and responsive AI capabilities.

## ProductGPT (Beta)
Revolutionize your e-commerce platform with AI that understands what your customers are searching for and recommends products accordingly. It's designed to boost customer satisfaction and increase sales by making product discovery seamless and personalized.
